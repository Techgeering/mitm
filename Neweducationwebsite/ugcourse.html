Computer Science & Engineering

Computer science is the study of computers and computational systems. It is a broad field which includes everything from the algorithms that make up software to how software interacts with hardware to how well software is developed and designed.

Computer science courses can be taken as a degree or diploma. Some topics covered in computer science courses include: Database system architecture Data models Relational database design Computer science is a fast-developing field with a wide range of career options. National intelligence agencies are always looking for graduates with the skills to create software and understand systems.

Mechanical Engineering

B. Tech Mechanical Engineering studies engineering concepts related to machine manufacturing, design, maintenance, and analysis. Furthermore, Btech Mechanical Engineering is a four-year undergraduate degree programme that will prepare you for a rewarding career that will benefit you and society.

Some of the top job roles after completing BTech Mechanical Engineering are mechanical engineer, assistant mechanical engineer, professor, automobile engineer, and technical sales engineer. Some of the top specializations in mechanical engineering are robotics, automobile, and thermal engineering.

Electrical Engineering(EE)

B. Tech with a major in Electrical Engineering is an undergraduate program that lasts for four years. It is the area of engineering that focuses on the study of applications of electronics, electricity and electromagnetism. The course also covers the fundamentals of electromagnetism and electronics.

The BTech Electrical Engineering syllabus broadens the learning vision and helps students to manage various domains under electrical engineering. It covers a range of subtopics including electronics, power, control systems, signal processing, and telecommunications.

Electrical and Electronics Engineering (EEE)

B. Tech EEE is a 4-year undergraduate degree programme in electrical and electronics Engineering. This course covers various topics about the application of electronics, electromagnetism, and the the function of various electrical components to develop new and advanced electrical equipment, etc.
